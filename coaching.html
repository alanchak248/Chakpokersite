Who is this coaching aimed at?

Anyone who wants to take their game to the next level. I’ve worked with players from 1/2 live to 1kNL online.

What can I expect from the coaching?

Before we start, I’ll listen to your goals and craft a lesson plan specific to your needs. Whether it’s theory or exploiting your player pool, we’ll work towards those goals efficiently.

What makes you different from other coaches?

Many coaches focus each lesson on one spot. I prefer teaching underlying concepts that transfer across spots. You’ll get a compact set of principles that scale across formats and situations.

Start with a single session. If it’s not a fit, you can stop there. You can also evaluate my free content on YouTube and review student feedback.
